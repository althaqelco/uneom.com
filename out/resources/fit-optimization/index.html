Accurate Measurement Techniques

Professional measurement techniques for accurate sizing

Essential Measurements

Upper Body

  • • Chest/Bust circumference
  • • Waist circumference
  • • Hip circumference
  • • Shoulder width
  • • Arm length
  • • Neck circumference

Lower Body

  • • Waist circumference
  • • Hip circumference
  • • Inseam length
  • • Outseam length
  • • Thigh circumference
  • • Rise measurement

Measurement Best Practices

Preparation

  • • Use proper measuring tape
  • • Ensure relaxed posture
  • • Wear appropriate undergarments
  • • Take measurements over thin clothing

Technique

  • • Keep tape parallel to floor
  • • Apply consistent tension
  • • Take multiple measurements
  • • Record immediately

Accuracy

  • • Double-check all measurements
  • • Use consistent measurement points
  • • Account for ease and comfort
  • • Consider garment style
