National Symbols in Design

Understanding Saudi national symbols and their appropriate use is essential for creating respectful, impactful patriotic uniforms:

The Saudi Flag Colors

Green represents Islam and prosperity; white symbolizes purity and peace

Design Applications:

  • Primary accent colors in uniforms
  • Tie and scarf options
  • Embroidery thread colors
  • Pin and badge designs
  • Uniform piping details

The Shahada & Sword

The Islamic declaration of faith and the sword of justice

Design Applications:

  • Subtle embroidery on formal wear
  • Pin badges for National Day
  • Printed scarves and ties
  • Special event accessories
  • Commemorative patches

The Palm & Crossed Swords

National emblem representing justice, strength, and growth

Design Applications:

  • Lapel pins and brooches
  • Embroidered pocket squares
  • Cufflink designs
  • Belt buckle accents
  • Formal button covers

Vision 2030 Logo

The Kingdom's transformation journey and future aspirations

Design Applications:

  • Modern uniform collections
  • Tech sector attire
  • Innovation team wear
  • Special project uniforms
  • Corporate event clothing

The Power of Green & White

Saudi Green

The distinctive green of the Saudi flag represents Islam, growth, and prosperity. It's the primary color for national celebrations.

Pantone: 349 C

Hex: #006C35

RGB: 0, 108, 53

Pure White

White symbolizes purity, peace, and the bright future of the Kingdom. It provides contrast and balance in patriotic designs.

Usage: Background, text, accents

Represents: Purity, peace

Pairing: Always with Saudi green

Best Practices & Ideas

Respect Cultural Symbols

Ensure all national symbols are used respectfully and appropriately

  • Consult cultural advisors
  • Follow official guidelines
  • Avoid placement on inappropriate items
  • Maintain dignity in all uses

Balance Pride & Professionalism

Patriotic expression should enhance, not replace, professional standards

  • Maintain industry-appropriate dress
  • Use accents rather than full transformation
  • Keep customer-facing roles polished
  • Allow personal expression within guidelines

Include All Employees

National celebrations should unite the entire workforce

  • Provide options for all roles
  • Consider size inclusivity
  • Respect individual preferences
  • Make participation welcoming, not mandatory

Quality Over Quantity

Invest in items that represent national pride well

  • Choose durable materials
  • Focus on versatile accessories
  • Select items usable year after year
  • Avoid cheap, disposable items

Document & Share

Capture celebrations for engagement and brand building

  • Professional photography
  • Social media campaigns
  • Internal communications
  • Year-over-year comparisons
