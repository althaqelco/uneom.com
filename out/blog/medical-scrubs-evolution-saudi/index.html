Infection Control Standards

Medical uniforms play a critical role in infection prevention. Saudi healthcare facilities follow strict protocols aligned with international standards:

Fabric Selection

  • Tightly woven to prevent pathogen penetration
  • Smooth surface that doesn't harbor bacteria
  • Withstands high-temperature washing (60°C+)
  • Compatible with hospital-grade detergents

Laundering Protocol

  • Daily uniform changes recommended
  • Hospital laundering preferred over home washing
  • Segregation of contaminated items
  • Proper bagging and transport procedures

Fit & Coverage

  • Appropriate coverage without excess fabric
  • Sleeves that allow proper hand hygiene
  • No dangling accessories or loose threads
  • Secure closures that stay fastened

Replacement Schedule

  • Regular inspection for wear and damage
  • Immediate replacement of torn items
  • Retirement after visible wear
  • Annual uniform refresh recommended

Department-Specific Guide

Emergency & Trauma

Requirements:

  • Fluid-resistant materials
  • High visibility colors
  • Reinforced construction
  • Quick-dry properties

Colors: Red or burgundy for quick identification

Features: Multiple pockets, ID badge loops, reinforced knees

Surgery & Operating Theater

Requirements:

  • Sterile-compatible
  • Low-lint fabric
  • Comfortable for long procedures
  • Temperature appropriate

Colors: Green or blue to reduce eye strain

Features: Close-fit cuffs, disposable options, antimicrobial

Nursing & Patient Care

Requirements:

  • Comfortable for 12-hour shifts
  • Easy movement
  • Professional appearance
  • Stain resistant

Colors: Navy, ceil blue, or department-specific

Features: Functional pockets, pen slots, badge holders

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Requirements:

  • Chemical resistant
  • Easy decontamination
  • Full coverage
  • Modest design

Colors: White or light colors to show contamination

Features: Snap closures, sleeve cuffs, lab coat options

Administration & Reception

Requirements:

  • Professional appearance
  • Comfortable for desk work
  • Brand representation
  • Modest options

Colors: Corporate colors aligned with hospital brand

Features: Tailored fits, modest cuts, elegant details
