Common Mistakes to Avoid

Underestimating Replacement Needs

Impact: Budget overruns mid-year

Solution: Build in 15-20% buffer for replacements, new hires, and damage

Ignoring Hidden Costs

Impact: Unexpected expenses

Solution: Include alterations, laundering, storage, and distribution in planning

Choosing Price Over Quality

Impact: Higher long-term costs

Solution: Calculate total cost of ownership including replacement frequency

Late Planning Cycle

Impact: Rush orders, premium pricing

Solution: Start planning 6 months before fiscal year

One-Size-Fits-All Approach

Impact: Poor fit, waste

Solution: Invest in proper sizing programs and size-inclusive options

Neglecting Employee Input

Impact: Low adoption, complaints

Solution: Survey employees on comfort, functionality preferences
