Case Study

Beauty Chain Standardization

How UNEOM unified a leading Saudi beauty salon chain with professional uniforms across 25+ locations

IndustryBeauty & Aesthetics
LocationMultiple cities across KSA
Project Duration4 months

Client Overview

A prominent Saudi beauty salon chain with 25+ locations across Riyadh, Jeddah, and other major Saudi cities. With over 300 staff members across various roles including beauticians, aestheticians, hair stylists, nail technicians, and reception staff, the chain needed a uniform solution that would:

  • Create a consistent brand image across all locations
  • Distinguish between different staff roles
  • Accommodate Saudi cultural considerations
  • Provide comfort for long working hours
  • Maintain a premium aesthetic aligned with their high-end positioning

Key Challenge

Prior to partnering with UNEOM, each location had its own approach to staff attire, resulting in inconsistent brand representation and varying levels of professionalism across the chain.

New standardized uniforms improved brand consistency across locations

Our Approach

1

Comprehensive Assessment

We conducted a thorough analysis of the client's brand identity, salon environments, staff activities, and cultural considerations. This included on-site visits to multiple locations to understand the unique needs of different roles.

2

Role-Specific Design

We developed distinctive yet cohesive uniform designs for each role, ensuring they met both functional requirements (comfort, ease of movement, stain resistance) and aesthetic standards aligned with the premium brand position.

3

Culturally-Appropriate Solutions

We designed options that respected Saudi cultural norms while maintaining style and function, including hijab-friendly designs, modest fits, and appropriate length options for all staff members.

4

Premium Fabric Selection

We selected high-quality, durable fabrics that could withstand the rigors of salon environments, including exposure to beauty products, frequent washing, and extended wear throughout long shifts.

5

Prototype Testing

Before full implementation, we conducted wear tests with staff members across different roles to gather feedback and make necessary adjustments to ensure comfort and functionality.

6

Phased Implementation

We developed a rollout plan that ensured minimal disruption to operations, with training for staff on proper wear and care of new uniforms, and a system for replacements and new hires.

The Solution

Role-Differentiated Uniform System

We created a comprehensive uniform system with clear role differentiation while maintaining visual cohesion across the entire staff:

  • Beauticians & Aestheticians:

    Modern tunics in the brand's signature purple with ergonomic design for ease of movement

  • Hair Stylists:

    Stylish black aprons with brand embroidery over color-coded shirts indicating experience level

  • Nail Technicians:

    Stain-resistant tops with specially designed sleeves that could be easily adjusted during detailed work

  • Reception & Front Desk:

    Elegant blazers and blouses in brand colors, creating a sophisticated first impression

Cultural Considerations & Brand Integration

Our designs thoughtfully incorporated Saudi cultural expectations while maintaining the brand's premium aesthetic:

  • Hijab-Friendly Designs:

    Custom-designed hijabs in brand colors with moisture-wicking fabrics for comfort during long shifts

  • Modest Yet Functional:

    Appropriate coverage while maintaining ease of movement for technical beauty procedures

  • Brand Identity Integration:

    Consistent use of brand colors, refined embroidery of logos, and custom buttons with brand insignia

  • Premium Finishing:

    High-quality stitching, reinforced seams, and premium fabrics that maintained appearance after repeated washing

Key Outcomes

  • Enhanced Brand Consistency:

    Uniform application of brand identity across all locations created a coherent visual experience for customers

  • Improved Staff Identification:

    Customers could easily identify staff roles, enhancing the service experience

  • Higher Staff Satisfaction:

    Comfortable, culturally-appropriate uniforms boosted employee morale and sense of professionalism

  • Streamlined Operations:

    Centralized uniform management reduced administration time and costs

  • Long-Term Durability:

    Premium fabrics and construction resulted in longer-lasting uniforms, reducing replacement frequency

  • Scalable System:

    The standardized system easily accommodated new locations as the chain continued to expand
