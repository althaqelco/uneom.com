Building Saudi Arabia's Uniform Industry

Our Story

Founded in 2004 in Riyadh, UNEOM began with a simple mission: to provide Saudi Arabia with world-class professional uniforms that combine quality, comfort, and style.

Today, we are proud to be the Kingdom's leading uniform manufacturer, serving over 500 clients across healthcare, corporate, hospitality, education, and industrial sectors.

With our modern manufacturing facilities, ISO 9001:2015 certification, and a dedicated team of 200+ professionals, we continue to set the standard for uniform excellence in Saudi Arabia and support the Kingdom's Vision 2030 goals.

About UNEOM FAQ

Common Questions

When was UNEOM established?

UNEOM was established in 2004 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. We have over 20 years of experience in uniform manufacturing and have grown to become the leading uniform supplier in the Kingdom.

How many clients does UNEOM serve?

UNEOM proudly serves over 500 clients across Saudi Arabia, including major hospitals, corporations, hotel chains, schools, and government institutions.

Is UNEOM ISO certified?

Yes, UNEOM is ISO 9001:2015 certified, demonstrating our commitment to quality management systems. We also hold OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification for our fabrics.

Where is UNEOM headquartered?

UNEOM's headquarters is located in Riyadh on King Fahd Road. We also have showrooms and service centers in Jeddah and Dammam to serve clients across the Kingdom.

What industries does UNEOM serve?

UNEOM serves multiple industries including healthcare (hospitals, clinics), corporate (banks, offices), hospitality (hotels, restaurants), education (schools, universities), industrial (factories, oil & gas), and security sectors.

Does UNEOM manufacture locally in Saudi Arabia?

Yes, UNEOM has modern manufacturing facilities in Saudi Arabia, supporting Saudi Vision 2030's localization goals. We produce over 100,000 uniform pieces monthly with a team of 200+ skilled professionals.
