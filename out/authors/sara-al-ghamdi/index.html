About Sara Al-Ghamdi

Sara Al-Ghamdi leads UNEOM's sustainability initiatives, bringing 10 years of expertise in eco-friendly textile development and sustainable uniform production. She specializes in developing uniform solutions that reduce environmental impact while maintaining performance standards and cost-effectiveness. Sara has implemented comprehensive sustainability programs throughout UNEOM's supply chain and manufacturing processes, focusing on water conservation techniques particularly relevant to Saudi Arabia's climate challenges. Her approach emphasizes circular economy principles in uniform lifecycle management.
