Why Does Choosing the Right Fabric Matter?

Students spend long hours in their school uniforms, so the fabric must be comfortable, durable, and healthy. The right fabric choice affects student comfort, academic performance, and self-confidence.

In Saudi Arabia's hot climate, fabric properties become even more important, as we need fabrics that breathe well and keep the body cool while maintaining an elegant appearance throughout the school day.
