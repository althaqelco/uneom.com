Importance of Safety Standards in Manufacturing

Safety standards play a pivotal role in the success of Saudi industrial sector. With Saudi Vision 2030 and the direction towards economic diversification, commitment to highest safety and quality standards has become essential.

Saudi Standards, Metrology and Quality Organization (SASO) sets the regulatory framework that ensures product safety and consumer protection, enhancing the competitiveness of Saudi industries locally and globally.

Fundamental Safety Standards

Four main pillars that form the foundation of safety in Saudi manufacturing

SASO Basic Standards

Saudi Standards, Metrology and Quality Organization sets fundamental safety standards

Basic Requirements:

  • Certified quality certificates
  • Periodic safety testing
  • Manufacturing process documentation
  • Continuous quality monitoring

Occupational Safety

Worker protection and ensuring safe work environment in all manufacturing stages

Basic Requirements:

  • Personal protective equipment
  • Continuous safety training
  • Emergency procedures
  • Periodic equipment inspection

Quality Assurance

Comprehensive systems to ensure product quality according to international standards

Basic Requirements:

  • ISO 9001 quality management system
  • Raw material testing
  • Production process monitoring
  • Final product inspection

Equipment Safety

Maintenance and operation of industrial equipment according to highest safety standards

Basic Requirements:

  • Regular preventive maintenance
  • Equipment calibration
  • Operator training
  • Early warning systems
