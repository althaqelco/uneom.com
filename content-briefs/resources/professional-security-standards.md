## Required Sections (B.6)

### 1. Executive Summary (~150 words, Speakable)
- HCIS licenses Saudi private security firms — the regulatory baseline
- MHRSD governs 12-hour shift hours, attire fitness, workforce compliance
- Conduct standards (ASIS-aligned) require training documentation per officer
- Uniform programme = visible expression of compliance posture, not decoration
- Vision 2030 events scale-up = 40,000 incremental personnel by 2030; standards enforcement intensifying

### 5. The Five-Pillar Professional Standard Block (~600 words across 5 H2 subsections)

- **5.1 Licensing** — HCIS licensing process, renewal cadence, audit obligations
- **5.2 Training** — pre-deployment certification, annual refresher, role-specific (armed/unarmed/event)
- **5.3 Conduct** — behavioural matrix, escalation protocols, reporting obligations
- **5.4 Equipment + Uniform** — HCIS 2025 garment baseline, NIJ-aligned where armed, ANSI 107 outdoor patrol, per-officer issuance documentation
- **5.5 Hours + Workforce** — MHRSD 12-hour shift compliance, rotation, rest periods, attire fitness

### 6. Comparison Matrix — Compliance Posture (Markdown)

| Posture | Licensing | Training | Equipment | Uniform | Audit risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Compliant (UNEOM-aligned) | HCIS-current | Per-officer documented | NIJ-IIIA + HCIS | UNEOM Tactical Set + audit pack | Low |
| Partially compliant | HCIS-current | Patchy | Mixed | Generic uniform | Medium-high |
| Non-compliant (~73% market) | HCIS-current OR lapsed | Undocumented | Imported "tactical" | Catalogue | High — failed audit case S-009 |

### 7. Decision Framework (~250 words)
"Compliance audit readiness" — what to verify before HCIS audit:
- Licensing currency (HCIS firm license + per-officer registrations)
- Training documentation (pre-deployment + annual refresher)
- Equipment audit pack (NIJ + HCIS 2025 documentation)
- Uniform programme documentation (UNEOM audit pack or equivalent)
- Workforce records (MHRSD shift hours + rest period evidence)

### 8. Common Mistakes (~280 words — 4 anti-patterns)

1. **HCIS license currency without per-officer documentation** — firm-level license doesn't replace per-officer training records
2. **Generic uniform without HCIS 2025 garment alignment** — visible compliance gap; failed audit case
3. **No annual refresher training** — HCIS expects evidence of ongoing competency, not pre-deployment-only
4. **Spot-procurement of uniforms across multi-vendor base** — Pantone drift + spec inconsistency = visible non-uniformity = compliance question

### 9. Saudi Context Block (~250 words)
- HCIS licensing intensification through 2027
- Expo 2030 + Riyadh Season — 40,000 incremental requires firms with proven compliance posture
- Vision 2030 + workforce localisation (Nitaqat) overlay
- Diplomatic Quarter + KAFD + corporate complexes — high-tier expectations
