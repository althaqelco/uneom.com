## Required Sections (B.6)

### 1. Executive Summary (~150 words, Speakable)
- Saudi uniform policy = HR + legal + procurement intersection; not a single document
- 8 mandatory components: scope, role grading, supply mechanism, replacement cadence, hijab/modesty inclusivity, conduct + violations, vendor/programme reference, signature page
- MHRSD workplace attire fitness governance
- Vision 2030 workforce localisation overlay (Nitaqat) for vendor preference clauses
- Industry-specific compliance (MoH, HCIS, GACA) integrated into the policy not appended

### 4. Compliance Citation Block (~280 words)
- **MHRSD — Workplace Attire Fitness** (the foundational citation)
- **Ministry of Commerce — Trademark + Brand-Use** (uniform brand-identity preservation)
- **Industry-Specific** — MoH, SFDA, HCIS, GACA, MoE, MoT (sector-specific embedment)
- **Saudi Council of Engineers** (where relevant — engineering attire)

### 5. The Eight Mandatory Components Block (~600 words across 8 H2 subsections — 75 each)

- **5.1 Scope** — who's covered, who's exempt
- **5.2 Role Grading** — uniform tiers per job role
- **5.3 Supply Mechanism** — UNEOM programme reference, sizing process, joiner-kit SLA
- **5.4 Replacement Cadence** — wash-count or calendar-driven, replacement-cost responsibility
- **5.5 Hijab + Modesty Inclusivity** — explicit clause, Pantone-matched variant available, no penalty
- **5.6 Conduct + Violations** — uniform-fitness expectations, modification restrictions, escalation matrix
- **5.7 Vendor / Programme Reference** — UNEOM contract specifics, audit-pack accessibility
- **5.8 Signature Page** — employee acknowledgement, policy revision date, manager sign-off

### 6. Industry-Specific Annex Block (~400 words)
What changes per industry:
- Healthcare → MoH/SFDA + AATCC 100 + ICMS protocol references
- Manufacturing → HCIS + NFPA 70E + UL ARC test references
- Aviation → GACA + NFPA 2112 + UL FR references
- Education → OEKO-TEX Class 2 + MoE child-safety references
- Hospitality → MOT + SFDA Food Service Worker Attire references
- Security → HCIS 2025 + NIJ-where-armed references
- Corporate → MC trademark + MHRSD attire fitness references
- Retail → MC trademark + MHRSD references

### 7. Decision Framework (~250 words)
"How to assemble the policy":
- Q1: Industry compliance scope → Annex selection
- Q2: Workforce composition → hijab inclusivity clause depth
- Q3: Vendor commitment → programme reference vs spot-procurement language
- Q4: Replacement-cost model → company-paid vs employee-cost-share

### 8. Common Mistakes (~280 words — 4 anti-patterns)

1. **Generic policy without industry-specific embedment** — fails procurement audit
2. **Hijab clause as afterthought** — visible exclusion of female workforce; legal exposure
3. **No supply mechanism reference** — policy + procurement disconnect; spot-buying creep
4. **No signature page** — unenforceable; HR audit gap

### 9. Saudi Context Block (~200 words)
- MHRSD enforcement intensifying through 2027
- Vision 2030 workforce localisation (Nitaqat) — vendor preference clause language
- Hijab-inclusive policy as default Saudi reality, not optional accommodation
- Industry-specific compliance enforcement expanding (HCIS 2025, GACA cabin updates)
