### 7. Decision Framework (~250 words)
"Which UNEOM healthcare garment for which role?":
- ER nurses → Classic Scrub Set (Premium tier in OR)
- OR teams → Premium Scrub Set + Surgical Gown
- Senior physicians → Doctor Lab Coat (Egyptian cotton)
- Pediatric ward → Class 2 OEKO-TEX scrubs
- Female clinical workforce → Hijab-integrated variants of all above

### 8. Common Mistakes (~280 words — 4 anti-patterns)

1. **Cotton-100% scrubs in ER** — antimicrobial efficacy decays at 30 cycles + autoclave 121°C cycles destroy cellulose; UNEOM bonded silver-ion poly-cotton 65/35 lasts 80+ cycles
2. **Buying scrubs by sticker price** — 110 SAR replaced every 8 months = 165 SAR/year; 180 SAR UNEOM lasts 14 months = 154 SAR/year
3. **Hijab "add-on" patch** — non-integrated retrofits fail Pantone, fit, and SFDA-registration tests; integrated variants are standard practice not premium add-on
4. **No audit pack** — MoH-licensed procurement requires documented per-batch AATCC + SFDA + SASO compliance evidence
