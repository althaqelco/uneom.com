### 5. The 14-Point Measurement Protocol (~600 words across 4 H2 subsections)

- **5.1 Upper Body (5 points)** — chest, shoulder width, sleeve length, back length, neck
- **5.2 Lower Body (5 points)** — waist, hip, inseam, outseam, thigh
- **5.3 Limb Detail (4 points)** — bicep, wrist, calf, knee
- **5.4 Hijab Variant Add-on (2 points)** — head circumference, modesty-grade neck clearance

### 6. Role-Graded Sizing Block (~400 words)
How sizing differs by role:
- Healthcare scrubs (XS–4XL standard + hijab variant)
- Aviation crew (12–15 piece set fitted to single Pantone batch — outlier handling critical)
- Security tactical (additional vest-mount geometry validation per fitting)
- Corporate executive (bespoke fit, half-canvas drape)
- Education K-12 (growth tolerance through 2 cm hem extension panels)

### 7. Comparison Matrix — Size Standards (Markdown table)

| Standard | Coverage | Granularity | Saudi-relevance |
|---|---|---|---|
| UNEOM 14-point | Full body + hijab | 14–16 points | Saudi-spec, all silos |
| Generic catalogue (XS–XL) | Body envelope only | 6–8 sizes | Outlier failure rate >18% in Saudi market |
| ISO 8559 | Body + accessory | 30+ points | Industry-rigorous, time-prohibitive >50 employees |
| Body-imaging 3D scanning | Full body envelope | 100+ points | Stunning data, but no live workforce dispatch SLA |

### 8. Decision Framework (~250 words)
"How to choose your fitting strategy" — flowchart:
- <50 employees → online size chart + sample-fit kit
- 50–200 → on-site UNEOM fitting team (Standard tier)
- 200–999 → dedicated fitting team (Pro tier)
- 1,000+ → multi-site simultaneous (Enterprise tier)

### 9. Common Mistakes (~280 words — 4 anti-patterns)

1. **Size-charting K-12 students at procurement time** — children grow 2.3 replacements/year. UNEOM 2 cm hem extension panels extend fit-life ~6 months without re-procurement.
2. **Ignoring hijab-variant fitting** — modesty-grade neck clearance + sleeve fit accommodating hijab base layer + extended hem are not optional for the female workforce. Catalogue uniforms missing these dimensions are visibly ill-fitting.
3. **Outlier dismissal** — XS or 4XL+ outliers represent ~5–8% of any Saudi workforce. Dismissing them as "exceptions" creates uniformed inconsistency. UNEOM custom-grades for documented outliers within standard programme cost.
4. **No digital record retention** — re-fitting on every replacement cycle is wasted time. UNEOM's 36-month digital record eliminates re-fit unless body change is reported.
