### 7. Decision Framework (~250 words)
"How to choose" — flowchart-rendered:
- Q1: Laundering profile? (institutional / home / dry-clean) → narrows to 3 family clusters
- Q2: Operating environment? (indoor stable / outdoor variable / hostile chemistry) → narrows further
- Q3: Compliance requirement? (none / OEKO-TEX / AATCC / NFPA / ANSI) → final filter
- Q4: Brand criticality? (Pantone ±5 OK / ±2 needed / ±1 critical) → tier selection within family

### 8. Common Mistakes (~280 words — 4 anti-patterns + physics-of-failure)

1. **Buying "antimicrobial" without AATCC 100 lab cert** — surface treatments wash out at 30 cycles vs bonded silver-ion's 80+. Lab certificate is the difference between marketing and engineering.
2. **Choosing fabric by GSM alone** — 320 GSM HRC2 FR is heavier and cooler than 180 GSM polyester for plant-floor use because fiber chemistry beats raw weight in heat-soak environments.
3. **Riyadh-spec fabric for Jeddah deployment** — dry-heat fabric chemistry fails under 90% RH coastal humidity within 4 months. Fabric must match anchor city.
4. **"Sustainable" fabric without duration validation** — a recycled-polyester polo that fails at 6 months is worse than a non-certified polo that lasts 18. Sustainability is duration first.

### 9. Saudi Context Block (~250 words)
The 3-climate-envelope thesis with vocab anchors:
- **Riyadh — الأربعينية + السموم** thermal swing → poly-cotton 65/35 + wool blend seasonal layering
- **Jeddah — النوّة + خماسين الصيف** humidity + salt-aerosol → mercerised wool-poly + brass fasteners + hydrolysis-resistant dyes
- **Dammam — موسم الخبر البحري** chloride → ripstop performance polyester + chloride-resistant hardware
- **Mecca/Medina — موسم الحج** compression → 120-cycle Hajj-grade
- **Tabuk — شتاء العشرة** cold → layered thermal regulation
- **Abha — برد عسير + ضباب السودة** mountain mist → water-repellent C0 + UV-rated reactive dye
