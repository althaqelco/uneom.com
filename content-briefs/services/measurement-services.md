## FAQs (5)

1. **هل توفّر UNEOM قياسات في موقع شركتي؟** — Yes — across all 24 Saudi cities. Mobile fitting teams dispatch within 24 hours (Riyadh), 48 hours (Tier-A cities: Jeddah, Dammam, Khobar, Mecca, Medina), or 72 hours (Asir, Tabuk, Jazan, smaller programme cities). Minimum: 50 employees per site engagement.
2. **كم نقطة قياس في عملية الـfit-grading؟** — 14 standardised points (chest, waist, hip, shoulder, sleeve, back, neck, bicep, wrist, inseam, outseam, thigh, calf, knee). Hijab variants add 2 points (head circumference + modesty-grade neck clearance) for 16 total. Per-employee measurement time: 8–12 minutes.
3. **هل تحتفظ UNEOM بسجلّ مقاسات الموظفين للاستبدال المستقبلي؟** — Yes — minimum 36-month digital record retention. Each employee record links: employee ID, 14-point dataset, role grade, hijab-variant flag, Pantone preference. Future replacements ship without re-fitting unless body-size change is reported via the programme dashboard.
4. **كم يستغرق قياس 200 موظف ميدانياً؟** — 2–3 working days for 200 employees (a single fitting team at 8–12 minutes per employee × 8-hour fitting day = ~50 employees/day). Pro and Enterprise tiers deploy multiple fitting stations in parallel for compressed timelines.
5. **هل تشمل القياسات متغيّرات الحجاب للنساء؟** — Yes — standard for all programmes including female workforce. Hijab-variant fitting includes head circumference + modesty-grade neck clearance + extended sleeve fit accommodating hijab base layer + extended hem. Pantone-matched hijab variant available across corporate, healthcare, education, and hospitality programmes.
