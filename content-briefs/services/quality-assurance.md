## FAQs (5)

1. **ما مراحل ضمان جودة الزي في UNEOM؟** — Five stages: fabric weight + weave validation, ISO 105 colour-fastness lab test, seam-strength QC on first 50 units, per-100-unit batch sampling, pre-ship inspection + serial assignment. Every garment carries the serial on its care label for traceability.
2. **كيف تختبر UNEOM ثبات لون القماش؟** — ISO 105 series testing in the in-house textile lab: wash fastness (105-C06), light fastness (105-B02), rub fastness (105-X12), and perspiration fastness (105-E04). Class 4 minimum required for institutional textiles; Class 5 for high-laundering programmes (hospitality, healthcare).
3. **ما الذي يغطّيه ضمان UNEOM 12 شهراً؟** — Fabric integrity (no premature pilling/thinning), dye stability (Pantone ±2 ΔE), seam integrity (no failure under normal load), embroidery durability (≥50 wash cycles). Healthcare adds antimicrobial retention; manufacturing adds FR retention. Excluded: third-party laundering damage, modification damage, ballistic impact.
4. **هل يحمل كل زي UNEOM رقماً تسلسلياً؟** — Yes — every garment ships with a unique serial imprinted on the care label. Serial maps to: production batch, fabric lot, QC inspector, dispatch date. Used by hospital programmes for wash-count tracking and by manufacturing programmes for ARC re-test schedules.
5. **متى أحتاج لـAudit-Pack tier بدلاً من الـStandard؟** — Whenever procurement is regulated: MoH-licensed hospitals (AATCC 100 + SFDA), Aramco/SABIC contractor lines (NFPA 70E + UL), GACA cabin operations (NFPA 2112 + UL), HCIS-licensed industrial security (NIJ + HCIS 2024). The audit-pack is required by the regulator's procurement audit, not optional.
