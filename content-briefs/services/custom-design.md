## FAQs (5)

1. **كم تستغرق دورة تصميم زي مخصّص؟** — 14 days from brief to first-fit sample (Starter tier). Pro and Enterprise tiers run 21–28 days because they include 2+ fit-sample loops, fabric mill engagement, and role-graded variants. The clock starts on signed brief; pause-time for client feedback isn't counted against SLA.
2. **ما الفرق بين التصميم المخصّص والتعديل على كتالوج؟** — Catalogue customisation = pick a UNEOM SKU, set Pantone + logo. Custom Design = engineer the garment from scratch around your brand, role grading, fabric envelope, and compliance specifics. Custom Design is required when catalogue silhouettes can't carry the brand or industry-specific compliance.
3. **هل يبقى الـtech-pack ملكاً لشركتي؟** — In Enterprise tier, yes — full IP transfer including pattern files, grading rules, fabric specifications. In Starter and Pro, UNEOM retains tech-pack ownership but provides perpetual production rights as long as the programme is active.
4. **كم تكلّف خدمة التصميم المخصّص؟** — 18,000–35,000 SAR Starter (single role); 35,000–80,000 SAR Pro (3–5 roles); 80,000+ SAR Enterprise (unlimited roles + fabric mill + IP). Design fee is separate from per-garment unit cost. Volume production typically pays back the design fee within the first 200–500 units.
5. **هل يمكن دمج زي رجالي ونسائي مع متغيّر حجاب؟** — Yes — and standard practice. UNEOM design teams work with hijab-coordinated variants (matching Pantone hijab + modesty-grade neckline + extended hem + sleeve fit accommodating hijab base layer) as native part of role grading, not an add-on retrofit.
