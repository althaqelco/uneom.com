## FAQs (5)

1. **ما برنامج استرجاع الزي القديم في UNEOM؟** — Closed-Loop tier service: UNEOM collects end-of-service-life garments from client sites annually (or triggered by rotation cycle). Garments are sorted: serviceable units alteration-rebadged for re-issue with cost recovery; end-of-life units recycled into industrial wipes for the manufacturing sector. The closed loop is documented for client ESG reporting.
2. **هل تستخدم UNEOM بوليستر معاد التدوير؟** — Yes — recycled-polyester variants available across hospitality (housekeeping ancillaries, chef ancillaries) and retail (polos, branded shifts) fabric families. Post-consumer recycled content: 30–50% depending on the specific fabric family. Verified through Global Recycled Standard (GRS) chain-of-custody.
3. **ما الفرق بين الزي المستدام والزي العادي؟** — Three engineered differences: (a) OEKO-TEX 100 chemical-safety certification (no restricted azo dyes, formaldehyde, heavy metals above thresholds); (b) recycled-polyester variants where fabric family supports; (c) end-of-life take-back closing the lifecycle loop. The most underestimated dimension: durability. A 14-month UNEOM rotation produces 43% less garment waste than an 8-month catalogue — that's the dominant sustainability lever.
4. **هل أزياء UNEOM معتمدة من OEKO-TEX؟** — Full UNEOM healthcare line is OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified (Class 2 child-skin-contact threshold for school programmes; Class 3 institutional for hospital programmes). Corporate + education programmes are progressively migrating to OEKO-TEX certification through 2027. Per-batch lab certificates available for procurement audit.
5. **كم تكلّف العلاوة على الـsustainable tier؟** — OEKO-TEX tier 0–3% premium vs non-certified equivalent (often zero where fabric family is already certified). Recycled tier 5–8% premium for recycled-poly. Closed-Loop tier 8–12% premium covering take-back logistics. Premium is typically offset by replacement-cycle extension — 14-month vs 8-month rotation is 43% less procurement spend, more than recovering the premium within year 1.
