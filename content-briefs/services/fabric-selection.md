## FAQs (5)

1. **ما القماش الأمثل لمناخ السعودية الصيفي؟** — There isn't one. Riyadh's dry-heat thermal swing demands different chemistry than Jeddah's 90% humidity coastal envelope or Dammam's 6,500 mg/m³ chloride zone. UNEOM stratifies by anchor city: Riyadh = poly-cotton 65/35 with thermal-swing-rated weave; Jeddah = mercerised wool-poly with hydrolysis-resistant dye; Dammam = ripstop performance polyester with chloride-resistant fasteners.
2. **كم نوع قماش متاح في كتالوج UNEOM؟** — 40+ fabrics in active rotation across the 8 fabric families: poly-cotton, Egyptian cotton poplin, HRC2 FR cotton, wool-poly tropical, performance polyester, cotton-spandex twill, OEKO-TEX bamboo, reflective hi-vis polyester. Each fabric carries documented test results against its target operating envelope.
3. **كيف يتمّ اختبار قماش جديد قبل دخوله برنامج UNEOM؟** — 5–7 day accelerated lab testing: humidity cycles equivalent to 18 months of Jeddah service, salt-spray cabinet at Dammam-equivalent chloride concentrations, heat-soak ramps to plant-floor or tarmac envelopes, wash-cycle compression. Pass thresholds documented per fabric family.
4. **هل يمكن اختيار قماش UNEOM لكل مدينة في برنامج متعدّد المواقع؟** — Yes — preferred for multi-city programmes. UNEOM ships city-spec fabric variants within a single programme contract, Pantone-locked across all variants for brand consistency. Riyadh stores receive thermal-swing-rated; Jeddah receives humidity-engineered; Dammam receives chloride-resistant. Same brand, different chemistry.
5. **هل هناك تكلفة منفصلة لاختيار الأقمشة لو لم نكن مع Custom Design؟** — Yes — standalone fabric evaluation 8,000–18,000 SAR for existing programmes seeking re-spec. Bundled with Custom Design service at no separate fee. Per-batch documented testing for regulated procurement is pass-through-cost from accredited labs (varies by test scope).
