## Negative Entity Frame (1)

### ❌ Procuring Hajj uniforms from off-the-shelf catalogue suppliers
**Why fails:** Catalogue hospitality fabrics are engineered for 60–80 wash cycles annually — the typical European/East Asian hospitality wear envelope. The Hajj 7-week compression delivers 120 cycles in two months. We have logged Mecca operators in Hajj 2023 that reached 40% fleet replacement within the first 5 weeks of season — uniforms visibly faded, dimensionally unstable, Pantone-drifted. Replacement stock was unavailable mid-season because the operator had not contracted advance production. **Solution:** UNEOM Mecca Programme = **120-cycle-rated Hajj-grade fabric + Q2-confirmed 21-day reserve production windows + daily replacement-ready depot stock during season**. The 7-week compression is predictable; planning for it is not heroic.
