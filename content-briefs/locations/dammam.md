## FAQs (5)

1. **أين أجد مصنّع زي يخدم البتروكيماويات والصناعات الثقيلة في الدمام؟** — UNEOM operates a Dammam depot serving 78+ Eastern Province accounts including Aramco contractor lines and SABIC affiliates. Standard fabric spec includes salt-spray cabinet validation per batch (6,500 mg/m³ chloride), chloride-resistant fasteners, and UL-tested NFPA 70E HRC2 FR rating where the role requires.
2. **ما تأثير الموسم البحري على الزي الصناعي؟** — August–September Gulf humidity peaks combine with chloride-aerosol to corrode generic zinc fasteners within 4 months and accelerate synthetic fabric pilling. UNEOM Dammam programmes ship brass or coated fasteners by default; standard polyester is replaced by ripstop performance polyester tested for chloride exposure.
3. **هل يخدم UNEOM مقاولي أرامكو وسابك؟** — Yes — across petrochemical contractor lines, plant-floor PPE, and adjacent service operations. NFPA 70E HRC2 FR coveralls + ANSI 107 Class 2 hi-vis integration is standard for high-risk roles. Per-batch UL test reports ship with every order.
4. **ما الفرق بين أقفال الزي العادية والمقاومة للكلوريد؟** — Generic zinc-coated zippers oxidize in 60–120 days under Eastern Province coastal exposure. Brass and electroplated chloride-resistant fasteners hold integrity past 18 months under the same conditions. Cost differential per garment is 8–14 SAR; replacement cost on a corroded coverall is 380+ SAR.
5. **ما زمن التسليم في الدمام والمنطقة الشرقية؟** — Joiner kits 48 hours from Dammam depot covering Dammam, Khobar, Dhahran, Jubail, Qatif, Ahsa. Repeat orders 14–21 days. New programme launches 28–45 days. Same-day urgent dispatch for documented contracts above 200 staff.

## Negative Entity Frame (1)

### ❌ Procuring inland-spec FR coveralls for Gulf-coast deployment
**Why fails:** A Riyadh-region NFPA 70E HRC2 coverall passes lab certification on FR rating but fails operational reliability in the Eastern Province coastal corridor — its zinc fasteners corrode within 4 months under chloride exposure, and the polyester reflective tape backing delaminates from chloride-aerosol penetration. We have logged Aramco-contractor accounts that lost 30% of their coverall fleet to non-FR-related failures within a single year. **Solution:** UNEOM Dammam Programme = same FR rating + chloride-resistant hardware + salt-spray-validated fabric. The FR certificate is necessary; it is not sufficient.
