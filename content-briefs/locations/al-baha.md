## FAQs (4)

1. **هل يخدم UNEOM فنادق الباحة الجبلية؟** — Yes — staged from the Jeddah depot with 72-hour delivery. Mountain-climate fabric specification adapted from the Abha programme: water-repellent C0 outer, thermal-blend inner, UV-rated reactive dye.
2. **ما الفرق بين مناخ الباحة وأبها؟** — Both are Sarawat Mountains highlands at ~2,200m elevation. Al-Baha has slightly drier overall conditions (less Sawda mountain mist than Abha) but similar thermal envelope. UNEOM ships the Abha-specification with localised Pantone reservation.
3. **هل يحتاج زي الباحة لقماش حراري شتوي؟** — Yes — winter overnight lows reach ~5°C. UNEOM Al-Baha programme includes thermal-blend variant for cold-weather rotation, similar to Abha specification.
4. **كم يستغرق التسليم في الباحة؟** — Joiner kits 72 hours from Jeddah depot. Repeat orders 14–21 days. Programme contracts above 100 staff receive dedicated dispatch coordination.
