## FAQs (4)

1. **هل بريدة تحتاج قماش شتوي خاصّ؟** — Yes — Qassim continental winter overnight reaches ~0°C with dry conditions that cause fabric brittleness in untreated polyester. UNEOM Buraidah programme uses thermal-blend variant for cold-weather rotation, similar to Tabuk-spec but milder envelope.
2. **ما تأثير برد القصيم على القماش؟** — Dry-cold winter conditions cause polymer-chain brittleness in untreated polyester (cracks under stress at -5°C). Cotton-100% loses thermal capacity when wet. UNEOM ships poly-cotton 65/35 with thermal-blend reinforcement for the winter quarter.
3. **هل تخدم UNEOM مدارس وجامعات القصيم؟** — Yes — Qassim University and regional MoE schools served via Riyadh ops with August-intake reserved window for school programmes. OEKO-TEX Class 2 child-safety standard.
