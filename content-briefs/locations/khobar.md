## Negative Entity Frame (1)

### ❌ Treating Khobar as a "milder" Eastern Province city than Dammam
**Why fails:** Chloride exposure does not respect city limits. Khobar's 6,200 mg/m³ vs Dammam's 6,500 mg/m³ is operational equivalence — both fail generic zinc hardware on the same 4-month timeline. We have logged Khobar accounts that contracted Riyadh-spec inland uniforms expecting "milder than Dammam" coastal exposure; corrosion failure rates landed within 5% of Dammam's. **Solution:** UNEOM ships the **full Dammam coastal-grade specification** to all Khobar accounts within 5 km of the Gulf coastline. The cost differential vs inland-spec is 8–14 SAR per garment; the cost of mid-deployment fastener replacement is 380+ SAR per coverall.
