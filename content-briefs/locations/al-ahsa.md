## FAQs (4)

1. **أين أجد مصنّع زي يخدم الأحساء؟** — UNEOM serves Al-Ahsa from the Dammam Metropolitan depot with 48-hour delivery to all addresses within Al-Ahsa Municipality. Eastern Province coastal-grade specification applies (chloride-resistant fasteners, hydrolysis-resistant dyes, ripstop performance polyester for industrial roles).
2. **ما تأثير مناخ واحة الأحساء على الأقمشة؟** — UNESCO oasis microclimate creates humidity pockets that stress untreated polyester (hydrolytic degradation within 90 days) and generic zinc fasteners (corrosion within 4 months). UNEOM Al-Ahsa programme uses Eastern coastal-grade specification: brass fasteners + ripstop performance polyester + reactive dyes hydrolysis-resistant.
3. **هل يخدم UNEOM فنادق الأحساء التراثية؟** — Yes — heritage hospitality properties within the UNESCO Heritage Zone served via Dammam depot. Mercerised wool-poly hospitality fabric calibrated for oasis humidity, MOT Hotel Classification compliance documented.
4. **ما الفرق بين زي الأحساء والظهران؟** — Operationally minimal — both are within the Eastern Province cluster served from the Dammam depot. Al-Ahsa adds the UNESCO oasis humidity-pocket variable; Dhahran is corporate-petrochemical-focused. Same fabric envelope; different anchor industries.
