## FAQs (8)

1. **ما اشتراطات GACA لزي طاقم الطائرة؟** — GACA Cabin Safety Standards align with NFPA 2112 / EASA equivalent: cabin crew evacuation-facing garments require ATPV ≥6 cal/cm² flame-resistance, static-dissipative ≤10⁹ Ω, and dimensional stability under emergency-slide deployment. Documentation per batch required.
2. **هل يجب أن يكون زي المضيف مقاوماً للحريق فعلاً، أم ادعاء؟** — Required by lab-certificate (UL or NFPA), not marketing. ~60% of regional FR-claim suppliers lack lab data. UNEOM provides per-batch UL test reports with every aviation shipment.
3. **كم يكلّف زي مضيف طيران كامل في السعودية في 2026؟** — Range 650–1,200 SAR per set (12–15 pieces). UNEOM premium tier 800–1,200 SAR; imported FR-claim 350–550 SAR (with verification gap); standard corporate-grade 200–300 SAR (non-compliant).
4. **لماذا ضمان 24 شهر بدلاً من 12؟** — Aviation crew sets contain 12–15 interdependent pieces. Single-piece failure forces full-set re-procurement (10× cost). 24-month warranty mathematically de-risks programme TCO; UL fabrics document 200+ industrial wash cycle retention.
5. **كم قطعة في زي مضيف طيران كامل؟** — 12–15 typical: blazer + 2 shirts + 2 trousers/skirts + scarf/tie + cardigan + apron + name badge + winter coat (seasonal) + footwear cover + gloves + hat + emergency-vest. UNEOM scopes per role (chief, junior, ground).
6. **هل UNEOM يخدم مطارات الدمام والمدينة؟** — Yes — KFIA Dammam and AMAA Madinah included in 24-city coverage. On-airport fittings for orders ≥80 staff with 28-day repeat lead time.
7. **ما الفرق بين «flame-retardant» و«inherent FR»؟** — *Flame-retardant treatment* is a chemical wash that washes out (~25–40 cycles). *Inherent FR* is woven fibre (e.g., para-aramid, modacrylic blend) — FR property is permanent. UNEOM aviation uses **inherent FR poly-cotton-aramid** for crew jackets — verified UL.
8. **هل يمكن تخصيص الألوان لشركة الطيران؟** — Yes — Pantone-locked dyeing with ±1 ΔE tolerance (brand-critical for airline visual identity). Programme includes thread-level brand control and embroidered insignia per role rank.

## Negative Entity Frame (2)

### ❌ ممارسة 1: Buying "FR-labelled" cabin uniforms without UL or NFPA certificate
**Why fails:** A surface flame-retardant chemical treatment passes a 5-second flame test in marketing demos but fails ATPV testing — the actual measure of evacuation-survivable thermal energy. ~60% of regional FR-claim suppliers we have audited cannot produce a UL test report. In a 2024 cabin incident, a treated polyester sleeve melted to skin within 0.3 seconds of contact. **Solution:** Require **UL-issued ATPV report per batch** + **NFPA 2112 conformance**. UNEOM ships every aviation order with the report attached.

### ❌ ممارسة 2: Single-set procurement, no piece-level replacement plan
**Why fails:** A cabin crew set has 12–15 interdependent pieces — Pantone-locked, role-graded. When one piece fails (typical first: shirt cuff at 8 months under high-rotation laundering), buyers without a piece-level replacement contract are forced to re-buy the whole set, including the 11 still-functional pieces, because Pantone batch-matching to year-old garments is impossible without supplier continuity. **Result: 10× the marginal cost** of a single-piece replacement. **Solution:** Programme contract with **Pantone-batch hold + 24-month piece-level replacement** built into the SOW.

## Compliance Citations (3)

### GACA — Cabin Safety Standards (Saudi alignment with EASA Part-CC)
> «Cabin crew garments shall provide demonstrable resistance to flash-fire ignition under standardized testing, retain dimensional integrity during emergency evacuation slide deployment, and be free of melt-drip behaviour at temperatures up to 200°C.»

### NFPA 2112 — Industrial Flash Fire (Aviation FR baseline)
> «Garments shall be certified to provide a minimum ATPV of 6 cal/cm² and tested per ASTM F1930 manikin assessment for total body burn ≤50% under a 4-second flash exposure.»

### SASO — Saudi Quality Mark, Aviation Workwear
> «Aviation operational uniforms shall conform to colour-fastness Class 4 minimum, tensile retention ≥85% after 100 wash cycles, and shall not contain restricted azo dyes per OEKO-TEX Standard 100.»
