## Negative Frames (2)
1. **Buying coveralls labeled "FR" without ATPV testing** — Why fails: chemical treatments wash out (~25 cycles), claim becomes unverifiable, ARC incident exposes worker. Solution: require ATPV ≥8 cal/cm² certificate per batch.
2. **Single-spec procurement (FR only, ignoring hi-vis or heat)** — Why fails: Saudi sites combine arc-flash + impact + 50°C — single-spec PPE fails one dimension. Solution: integrated HRC2 + ANSI 107 Class 2 + breathable construction.
