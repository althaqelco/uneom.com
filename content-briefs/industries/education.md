## FAQs (8)

1. **ما اشتراطات وزارة التعليم لزي المدارس السعودية؟** — MoE policy aligns with school-level governance; private and international schools set per-school specifications subject to MoE general guidance on modesty, sportswear safety, and visible identity. SASO Quality Mark and OEKO-TEX 100 are the textile baselines UNEOM applies by default.
2. **ما متوسط استبدال زي المدرسة سنوياً بسبب النموّ؟** — 2.3 replacements/year per primary student (UNEOM benchmark). Secondary students stabilise to ~1× replacement. UNEOM 2-cm hem extension panels extend primary fit life by ~6 months — material economic impact on family.
3. **هل قماش الزي المدرسي آمن للأطفال (OEKO-TEX)؟** — UNEOM ships only OEKO-TEX Standard 100-certified fabric: no AZO dyes >30 mg/kg, no restricted phthalates, no formaldehyde >75 mg/kg. Imported uncertified uniforms have failed Saudi customs random testing on chemical-safety grounds — not a hypothetical risk.
4. **كم يكلّف طقم زي مدرسي كامل في السعودية في 2026؟** — Range 95–180 SAR per set (UNEOM): primary 95 SAR, secondary 130 SAR, sportswear 180 SAR. Catalogue 60–110 SAR (chemical-safety + durability gap). Cheap imports 35–70 SAR (high failure rate).
5. **متى يُسلَّم زي المدرسة قبل بداية العام الدراسي؟** — UNEOM holds August lead-time slots for school programmes contracted by Q2. Standard repeat orders: 14 days. New designs: 21–35 days. The August–September window concentrates 65% of annual education orders — early contracting protects delivery SLA.
6. **هل يوفّر UNEOM التخصيص بشعار المدرسة؟** — Yes — twin-needle embroidery + Pantone-locked dyeing. School logo durability spec: 50+ wash cycles. Heat-press is not used (4–6 cycle failure).
7. **هل تخدم برامج المدارس الدولية في السعودية؟** — Yes — UNEOM has shipped to international schools across Riyadh, Jeddah, and Khobar. Programmes cover Pre-K through IB / A-level, with sportswear and house-system colour grading.
8. **ما الضمان وما يُستثنى؟** — 12 months covering fabric integrity, embroidery durability, and dimensional stability. Excluded: growth-driven re-fitting (handled via re-issue programme), modification damage.

## Negative Entity Frame (2)

### ❌ ممارسة 1: Buying chemical-safety-uncertified uniforms for K-12 students
**Why fails:** OEKO-TEX Standard 100 isn't a marketing label — it is the chemical-safety baseline that ensures restricted azo dyes (carcinogenic), formaldehyde (allergen), and heavy-metal residues sit below medically-validated thresholds for skin contact in children. Imported uncertified uniforms have failed Saudi customs random tests. The cost gap (UNEOM 95 SAR primary vs cheap import 35 SAR) is 60 SAR — meaningful per family, but the chemical-safety risk to a 7-year-old wearing the garment 6+ hours/day is not a fair trade. **Solution:** require **OEKO-TEX 100 certificate per fabric batch** + SASO Quality Mark.

### ❌ ممارسة 2: Last-minute August procurement at spot pricing
**Why fails:** **بداية الترم** rush concentrates 65% of orders in 8 weeks — a stock-and-pricing crunch that catalogue suppliers cannot handle. Schools that contract UNEOM in Q2 receive August delivery at locked Q1 pricing; schools that procure in late August pay +18% spot rates and face 30–50% partial-delivery risk. **Solution:** **Q2 annual contract** with August lead-time hold.

## Compliance Citations (3)

### MoE — School Uniform Governance Guidance
> «Schools shall ensure student attire is appropriate to learning environment, safe for daily wear, and conforms to general modesty and identity standards. Specific specifications are set at school level subject to general guidance.»

### SASO — Saudi Quality Mark, Children's Textiles
> «Textile articles intended for use by children shall conform to colour-fastness Class 4 (ISO 105), tensile retention ≥85% after 30 wash cycles, and chemical safety per OEKO-TEX Standard 100 or equivalent.»

### OEKO-TEX Standard 100 — Skin Contact Class II (Children)
> «Articles for use by children shall not contain restricted azo dyes ≥30 mg/kg, formaldehyde ≥75 mg/kg, or heavy-metal residues exceeding specified limits. Independent lab testing required per fabric batch.»
