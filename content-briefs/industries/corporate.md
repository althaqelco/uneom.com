## FAQs (8)

1. **ما تكلفة برنامج زي إداري لـ500 موظف في السعودية؟** — Baseline 600,000 SAR/year for 1,000 staff (UNEOM scoping). For 500: ~300,000 SAR with executive (10%), mid-tier (60%), shift (30%) mix. Single-programme contracts protect against the 20% Q4 surcharge spot-buyers face.
2. **كيف نخطّط ميزانية زي الشركات سنوياً؟** — Three phases: (a) Q1 baseline scoping (employees by role grade), (b) Q2 contract pricing lock with annual schedule, (c) Q3–Q4 staged delivery against schedule. The 30% planning rate today vs 75% quarterly purchase is the gap UNEOM closes via Programme Management service.
3. **ما الفرق بين تطريز chain-stitch وtwin-needle؟** — Chain-stitch is single-thread interlocking (front + back), fails at ~12 cycles when one thread breaks. Twin-needle locks two threads into a polyester foundation; UNEOM logo embroidery survives 50+ industrial cycles with measured ΔE colour stability.
4. **كم تستغرق دورة بدلة تنفيذية مفصّلة؟** — 21–35 days new tailoring (concept → fit-sample → bulk → delivery). 21 days for repeat orders against an existing pattern. Includes on-site fitting if order ≥80 staff.
5. **هل يحتاج زي الشركات إلى اعتماد حكومي محدّد؟** — No specific regulatory body, but: SASO Quality Mark for textiles is recommended; MHRSD workplace standards apply for safety-relevant roles (warehouse, plant); Ministry of Commerce trade-mark rules govern logo use. UNEOM ships compliance brief with each programme.
6. **ما الفرق بين الزي الإداري والـbusiness casual؟** — Corporate uniform = enterprise-issued, brand-coherent, HR-managed (logo, role-graded). Business casual = employee-purchased, dress-code-governed (e.g., closed-collar, no jeans). UNEOM serves the first category; corporate dress codes are out of scope.
7. **هل يوفّر UNEOM إدارة برنامج كاملة (re-issue, exit-return)؟** — Yes — Programme Management service covers: new-hire issue, re-issue cadence (annual/role-based), exit-return logistics, alteration repair, and pantone batch hold for year-over-year continuity.
8. **هل يمكن دمج 6 موردين في برنامج واحد؟** — Yes — typical 18% TCO reduction post-consolidation (UNEOM banking case S-005). Pre-merger: 6 vendors, 4 colour drifts, 7 lead-time variances. Post-merger: 1 programme, 1 Pantone batch, 21-day standardised lead.

## Negative Entity Frame (2)

### ❌ ممارسة 1: Quarterly spot-buying without annual contract
**Why fails:** 75% of Saudi enterprises buy quarterly, only 30% plan budgets — the 45% gap reaches Q4 demand peak (موسم الميزانيات) and pays a 20% premium. On a 600,000 SAR baseline, that's 120,000 SAR of lost margin annually, with no Pantone continuity (each spot-buy drifts ±5 ΔE) and no embroidery batch hold. **Solution:** Annual contract pricing lock + staged delivery + Pantone batch reservation.

### ❌ ممارسة 2: Optimising suit price without measuring embroidery durability
**Why fails:** A 700 SAR suit with chain-stitch logo lasts ~12 wash cycles before logo distortion. Distorted logo = brand damage that the employee carries through 50–100 client meetings. The "savings" of 500 SAR/suit becomes a brand-equity tax across 500 employees — measurably reducing client-side perception within 4 months. **Solution:** Twin-needle embroidery + polyester thread + Pantone-locked dye = 50+ cycle durability + 12-month warranty.

## Compliance Citations (3)

### Ministry of Commerce — Trade-Mark & Brand Use Rules
> «Commercial entities shall ensure brand identity elements (logos, colour systems, typography) used on employee garments conform to registered trademark specifications, with no unauthorized derivation or colour deviation that misrepresents the registered mark.»

### MHRSD — Workplace Attire Standards
> «Employers shall provide attire suitable to role function, with safety-relevant categories (warehouse, plant, field) requiring documented protective specifications. Office-administrative attire shall maintain professional appearance consistent with sector norms.»

### SASO — Saudi Quality Mark, Institutional Textiles
> «Corporate-issued textile articles shall conform to colour-fastness Class 4 minimum and tensile retention ≥85% after 50 wash cycles per ISO 105 series.»
