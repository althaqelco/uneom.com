## Required Sections (B.7)

### 1. Hero Before/After Slider
Before: standard mid-tier polo. After: premium cotton-spandex twill with metallic-thread embroidery + brand-coordinated accent strip.

### 2. Challenge Block (~200 words)
14-store retail chain repositioning from mid-tier to premium category. Mid-tier polos signalled mid-tier expectation to clients; merchandising and pricing repositioning needed visible signal alignment. Customer-experience surveys showed perceived-value gap between merchandising lift and staff presentation. Catalogue-grade polos (130 SAR avg) wouldn't carry the premium repositioning.

### 3. Approach Block (~250 words)
- **UNEOM Retail Staff Polo Premium variant:** cotton-spandex twill 200 GSM with metallic-thread Pantone-locked embroidery
- **Brand-coordinated accent strip** (Pantone-locked) across all 14 stores
- **Custom Design 21-day concept-to-sample** for premium-positioning fabric variant
- **Programme contract** with replacement-cycle SLA + quarterly review

### 4. Solution Detail (~250 words)
- **Fabric premium upgrade:** cotton-spandex twill 200 GSM (vs catalogue 160 GSM polyester)
- **Embroidery upgrade:** metallic-thread twin-needle Pantone-locked (50+ wash cycles vs catalogue 12-cycle chain-stitch)
- **Accent strip:** brand-coordinated colour-block across collar
- **Pantone-locked across 14 stores** for cross-store consistency

### 5. Numbers Block (Speakable)
- **14 stores** repositioned
- **180 staff** uniformed
- **+28% client-perceived premium positioning lift** in 6-month survey (post-deployment)
- **+15 SAR per polo** premium fabric upgrade vs prior catalogue
- **Pantone ±2 ΔE** cross-store consistency

### 6. Quote (only if consent)

### 7. Lessons Learned
1. Uniform fabric upgrade signals brand-tier shift visibly — clients perceive cotton-spandex as premium, polyester as mid-tier within seconds
2. Metallic-thread embroidery is the procurement-affordable premium signal (vs cheap chain-stitch)
3. Cross-store Pantone consistency is the brand-equity dimension repositioning depends on
