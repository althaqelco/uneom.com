## Required Sections (B.7 — full 9-section blueprint)

### 1. Hero Before/After Slider + breadcrumbs
- Image slider: before (4 different catalogue scrub variations) → after (single Pantone-locked UNEOM dusty-rose with gold accent, 4 role-specific cuts)
- Headline: "80 staff · 21 days · 46% client-confidence increase"

### 2. Challenge Block (~200 words)
The clinic's 4-year growth from 12 → 80 staff across 4 role categories created a visible inconsistency in a service category where presentation IS the product. Three different catalogue suppliers meant three different "white", three different cuts, three different fading patterns. Post-visit surveys at month 12 showed declining client-confidence scores; the marketing team correlated visual presentation with the trend. Fragmented vendor base meant 14-day Pantone drift across re-orders, no role-grading, no replacement programme.

### 3. Approach Block (~250 words)
UNEOM scoped the engagement as a Custom Design + Bulk Ordering integration:
- 14-day brand audit + concept design
- Custom dusty-rose-with-gold-accent colourway, Pantone-locked single batch
- 4 role-specific cuts (consultants, nurses, aestheticians, reception)
- Programme contract with joiner-kit SLA, replacement-cycle planning
- 21-day end-to-end concept-to-delivery (compressed from standard 28-day for dedicated production block)

### 4. Solution Detail Block (~250 words)
The fabric envelope:
- **Consultants:** Egyptian Giza-86 cotton poplin lab coats with embroidered name + specialty
- **Nurses:** UNEOM Premium scrub set in dusty-rose with gold piping (Pantone-locked)
- **Aestheticians:** Customised cotton-spandex twill with structural apron pockets for procedure tools
- **Reception:** Mercerised wool-poly tropical with brand-coordinated silk scarf

### 5. Numbers Block (the Result Stats — Speakable schema)
- **46% increase** in client-reported brand confidence at 6 months
- **3× reduction** in uniform-related complaints (fit, fading, inconsistency)
- **22 weeks longer** mean garment lifespan (vs previous catalogue scrubs)
- **21 days** concept-to-delivery total programme time
- **4 role categories** Pantone-locked across all 80 staff

### 6. Quote from Client (testimonial — only if approved)
**Required:** named clinical operations director with title + facility name, full written consent. Quote 40–80 words, focus on programme operations transformation. **Omit section if not secured — do not fabricate.**

### 7. Lessons Learned Block (~150 words — 3 takeaways)
1. **Visible inconsistency is a measurable revenue lever** — multi-vendor scrub bases erode client-confidence scores in service-category healthcare
2. **Role grading is brand engineering** — the right uniform per role amplifies, the wrong uniform erodes; one-size-fits-all is an anti-pattern
3. **Programme contracts beat spot procurement** — Pantone hold + replacement cadence eliminates the 6-month cliff that catalogue-based programmes hit
