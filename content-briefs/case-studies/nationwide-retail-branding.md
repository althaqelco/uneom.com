## Required Sections (B.7)

### 1. Hero Before/After Slider
Before: 4–7 vendor catalogue inconsistency across stores. After: single Pantone-locked UNEOM polo across all 40 stores.

### 2. Challenge Block (~200 words)
National retail chain (40 stores) sourcing from 4–7 vendors across 12 Saudi cities. Pantone drift ±5 ΔE store-to-store. Lead times 30–60 days from each vendor unsynchronised. Q4 spot-pricing premium 20% during موسم الميزانيات. Joiner kits handled ad-hoc per store — typical lag 2–3 weeks for new hires. Brand-recognition surveys showed declining inter-store consistency scores; HQ marketing flagged as a priority.

### 3. Approach Block (~250 words)
- **Programme contract:** annual locked pricing, single-batch fabric hold for 18 months
- **Pantone-locked single batch** across all 40 stores
- **48-hour joiner-kit SLA** from city-nearest depot (Riyadh + Jeddah + Dammam Metro)
- **14-day repeat lead** post-launch for any store
- **Quarterly programme review** with chain HQ
- **Replacement-cycle SLA** included

### 4. Solution Detail (~250 words)
- **Fabric:** UNEOM Retail Polo (cotton-spandex twill 200 GSM, 200+ wash cycle validated, ISO 105 Class 4 fastness)
- **Embroidery:** Twin-needle Pantone-locked logo (50+ wash cycle durability)
- **Logistics:** 3-depot national coverage (Riyadh + Jeddah + Dammam Metro)
- **Audit pack:** SASO Quality Mark per batch + Pantone batch certificate + lead-time documentation

### 5. Numbers Block (Speakable)
- **40 stores** unified across **12 Saudi cities**
- **18% TCO reduction** vs prior multi-vendor base
- **48-hour joiner-kit SLA** every store (24-hour for Riyadh)
- **Pantone ±2 ΔE** consistency vs prior ±5 ΔE drift
- **14-day repeat lead** post-launch
- **20% Q4 premium eliminated** via annual contract

### 6. Quote from Client
National retail chain operations director (with consent). Otherwise omit.

### 7. Lessons Learned
1. Multi-city Pantone consistency erodes silently — chain HQ doesn't see drift store-by-store, but customers travelling between cities do
2. Annual contract pricing eliminates the موسم الميزانيات spot-premium that catalogue procurement absorbs
3. 3-depot national coverage (Riyadh + Jeddah + Dammam) covers 12-city footprint with consistent 24–48-hour SLA
